About

I'm a 3rd-year Robotics & Automation Engineering student at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, Coimbatore — specialising in autonomous systems, embodied AI, and the full robotics software stack from perception to control.

My work spans ROS/ROS2 navigation, visual SLAM, UAV autonomy, imitation learning, and computer vision — built across real hardware: NVIDIA Jetson, Raspberry Pi, Pixhawk, ZED2i, and LeRobot S101 with NVIDIA Thor.

> Currently engineering a commission-based autonomous robot
  for Daimler India (DICV) — autonomous AC cassette servicing
  for their Chennai manufacturing plant.

I'm interested in roles at the intersection of robotics software engineering and AI research — where systems actually move, perceive, and act in the physical world.
